# QR8

**Mixed-Precision QR on INT8 Tensor Cores**

Low-bit start. High-precision finish.

QR8 is a reproducible artifact for QR factorization with INT8 initialization, FP64 refinement, GPU performance experiments, and paper-supporting data.

## Authors

Nima Sahraneshinsamani, José I. Aliaga, Sandra Catalán, and José R. Herrero.
